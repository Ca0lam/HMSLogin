This is a step by step guide, for logging in to your account

Step 1: Type in your name, it should be more than six characters, but less than fourteen characters

Step 2: Then you type in your password it also should be more than six characters, but less than fourteen characters

Step 3: If you don't have an account you can register by clicking the register button under the login button
